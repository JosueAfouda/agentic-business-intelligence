## YOUR TASK

Produce a SHORT and PRECISE business-oriented analysis composed of TWO sections:

# Insights

* Provide a concise analytical commentary on the results.
* Highlight meaningful patterns, trends, rankings, concentrations, or anomalies visible in the data preview.
* Focus strictly on what can reasonably be inferred from the dataset.
* If the dataset is small or limited, explicitly acknowledge this limitation.
* Do NOT restate raw data mechanically.
* Do NOT speculate beyond the available evidence.

# Recommended Actions

* Provide practical and realistic next steps aligned with the original business question.
* Actions must be proportionate to the evidence.
* If the data is insufficient to support strong conclusions, recommend further analysis instead of bold decisions.
* Keep recommendations actionable but not over-prescriptive.

---

## ANTI-HALLUCINATION RULES (STRICT)

You MUST:

* Use ONLY the information explicitly provided above.
* Base all insights on the dataset preview and metadata.
* Avoid inventing trends not visible in the preview.
* Avoid assuming time coverage unless clearly visible.
* Avoid assuming causality.
* Avoid assuming business context not present in the question.
* Avoid fabricating percentages, growth rates, or comparisons not computable from the provided data.
* If uncertainty exists, state it clearly.

If the dataset is too small or partial to draw strong conclusions, explicitly state:
"The available data is limited and does not allow strong conclusions."
